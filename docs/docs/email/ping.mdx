If you prefer to set up email in your codebase to have full control when rendering emails, Ping is probably for you!

Ping allows you to receive a webhook when MagicBell recommends sending an email based on MagicBell's [smart functionality](https://magicbell.com/docs/smart-delivery). You're getting the benefits of MagicBell, yet have complete control over every email.

## Deliver email notifications with a ping

To configure ping for your MagicBell project, follow these steps:

1. From the **Settings > Providers** page in your dashboard, select Ping.
1. Enable the provider and enter the destination URL for your ping then click **Save**.
